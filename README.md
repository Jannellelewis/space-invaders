# space-invaders

Game Description: Aliens are attacking the player but the player can move left and right to avoid the aliens. the player can fire bullets at the enemy but everytime it hits the enemy speeds up. there are also shields that protect the player but those can lose health if hit by any bullet. 

Extensions: 
1st: adds shields spread across the screem that weaken everytime its hit by a bullet

2nd: the shields are actually drawn and visible, the go from green to red as they lose health

3rd: everytime an alien is hit by a bullet it speeds up
